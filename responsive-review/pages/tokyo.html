Welcome to Tokyo

Welcome to Tokyo, a vibrant metropolis where tradition meets innovation! Our travel agency is excited to offer you the opportunity to explore this dynamic city in the heart of Japan. Tokyo is a city like no other, where towering skyscrapers and ancient temples coexist harmoniously. Get ready to experience a blend of modern marvels and timeless cultural treasures.

During your visit to Tokyo, immerse yourself in the bustling energy of neighborhoods like Shibuya and Shinjuku, where neon lights illuminate the streets and shopping districts buzz with activity. Don't miss the iconic Shibuya Crossing, one of the busiest intersections in the world, or the tranquil beauty of Meiji Shrine nestled in the heart of Yoyogi Park.

Discover Tokyo's rich history by visiting historic sites such as the majestic Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa, Japan's oldest temple, or the Imperial Palace with its beautiful gardens and historic significance. Explore the unique fashion and pop culture scene in Harajuku, where you'll find quirky shops and colorful street fashion.

For food enthusiasts, Tokyo is a culinary paradise offering a wide array of delicious dishes. Indulge in sushi at the famed Tsukiji Fish Market, savor authentic ramen noodles in hidden alleyways, or enjoy freshly made tempura and yakitori. Tokyo's food scene is sure to tantalize your taste buds.


Things To Do


  • Mt. Fuji - As Japan's highest peak, the legendary Mt. Fuji (Fuji-san) stands 12,388 feet (3,776 meters) tall.
  • Lake Ashi - In the shadow of Mount Fuji, Lake Ashi (Ashi-no-ko), is a scenic spot in Hakone National Park. Considered sacred by the Japanese, it is home to the famous Hakone Shinto shrine.
